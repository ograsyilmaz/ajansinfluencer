What is Influencer?

Influencer marketing (also influence marketing) is a form of marketing in which focus is placed on influential people rather than the target market as a whole. It identifies the individuals that have influence over potential customers, and orients marketing activities around these influencers.

Why working with influencers ?

Social reach: They can reach millions of people with social media. Original content: Influencers are original, creative, unusual and marketing purposes. Consumer confidence: Strong relationships with influencers mass audience; followers rely on influencers’ ideas.
